About

Hello! I am a researcher specialized in artificial intelligence and computer vision for earth observation.

I hold a PhD from Onera (the French aerospace lab) and CentraleSupelec on anomaly detection for Synthetic Aperture Radar images. I now work at Geowatch Labs on satellite image analysis for agriculture monitoring. My expertise is focused on developing self-supervised learning AI models for tasks such as detection, classification, feature extraction, image denoising and super-resolution.
